About Us

What is IT Capstone Archive?

This system simplifies capstone management through clearly defined role-based functionalities for teachers, students, and administrators. It addresses the difficulties teachers face when manually searching through physical thesis documents by providing a centralized digital repository for quick and organized access. Furthermore, the system enables students to easily view previously submitted theses, allowing them to gain valuable insights, avoid topic duplication, and align their research with academic standards.

Culminating Project

The IT Capstone project is typically the final, major project that students undertake in their IT program.

Archive and Access

The IT Capstone Archive provides a way to preserve these projects for future reference, research, and potential reuse.

Real-world Application

The project often involves solving a real-world IT problem or developing a software or hardware solution.

Diverse Projects

On how to Use?

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

Users begin by either signing up with an email and password or logging in using Google. They are also prompted to select their user role: Student, Teacher, or Admin. CAPTCHA is implemented to ensure secure login.

  • Sign up with email and password or log in with Google
  • Select your user role: Student, Teacher, or Admin
  • CAPTCHA protection for secure login

Step 2: Access Personalized Dashboard

After logging in, users are directed to a dashboard tailored to their role. Students can view their profile and submitted capstones, while teachers see capstone submissions for review. Admins have access to manage users and monitor system statistics.

  • Students can view profile info and submitted capstones
  • Teachers see submitted capstones for review
  • Admins manage users and view system stats

Step 3: Submit or Manage Capstone Projects

Students can upload their capstone title, abstract, and document for submission. Teachers review these submissions, providing approval or comments. Admins oversee all capstone submissions and manage the overall workflow.

  • Students upload capstone title, abstract, and document
  • Teachers review, approve, or comment on submissions
  • Admins oversee all submissions and system activity.

Step 4: Interact with Capstone Documents

Users can view, download, and manage capstone documents directly from the app. Students and teachers can access the documents in-browser or download them for further review. Action buttons allow users to manage submissions, including editing or taking other necessary actions.

  • View documents in-browser
  • Download capstone files
  • Manage submissions with edit or action buttons.
